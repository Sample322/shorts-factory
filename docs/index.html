Shorts Factory

Shorts Factory is a local desktop/web tool that helps a creator turn user-provided long-form videos into short vertical clips, generate captions and metadata, and publish finished clips to the creator's connected accounts.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service
Runs locally

The editing pipeline runs on the creator's own computer. The public website is used only for app information, policy pages, and OAuth redirect support.

User-controlled publishing

The creator must connect their own account and explicitly enable platform upload before any finished clip is sent to TikTok or YouTube.

Creator-provided media

Users are responsible for using source videos, music, and other assets they own or have permission to use.
